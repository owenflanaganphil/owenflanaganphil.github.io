Philosophy

Owen Flanagan is the James B. Duke University Professor Emeritus of Philosophy and Professor of Neurobiology Emeritus at Duke University. With a Ph.D. from Boston University, he has taught at Wellesley College and Duke University, where he has made significant contributions to the fields of philosophy of mind, ethics, and cross-cultural philosophy. His work integrates insights from psychology, neuroscience, and anthropology, offering a comprehensive approach to understanding human consciousness and moral psychology1. Owen Flanagan is also a Faculty Fellow in Cognitive Neuroscience and Co-Director of the Center for Comparative Philosophy.
