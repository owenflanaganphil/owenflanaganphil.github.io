I believe that education is not merely a preparation for life but life itself. I hope that students do not just memorize certain facts about what happened in the history of philosophy, but are able to apply the ethical considerations learned in the classroom in their daily lives. I emphasize projects-based learning rather than exams-based learning where students can build something of their own and I give them the freedom to present on non-traditional media.
